

# Raspberry Coffee cake

Modified from the original at (http://omgchocolatedesserts.com/raspberry-cream-cheese-coffee-cake/)

## Ingredients

### Filling
170 grams cream cheese-softened
45 g sugar
1 egg white
150 grams frozen raspberries

### Cake 
200 g all-purpose flour
½ teaspoon baking powder
½ teaspoon baking soda
¼ teaspoon salt
80 g Tablespoons unsalted butter- softened
1 dl cup sugar
1 egg
1 egg yolk
1 teaspoon vanilla extract
1 dl  sour labneh
1 dl yoghurt

### Streusel topping 
30 g sugar
35 g flour
45 g butter - chilled and cubed

## Instructions
* Preheat oven to 350F.
* To make the filling: Mix together the cream cheese and sugar on medium-low speed until creamy. Add eeg white end mix on low just to combine. Set aside.
* To make the cake batter: In a bow stir together flour, baking powder, baking soda and salt, set aside.
* With an electric mixer cream together the butter and sugar on medium-low speed. Add egg and egg yolk and vanilla. Slowly mix in the flour mixture alternating with the labneh and yoghurt. Transfer the batter into a 6 inch aluminium form and smooth with a spatula. Spread the cream cheese filling on top. Place raspberries onto cream cheese filling.
* To make the topping: Combine sugar, flour and chilled cubed butter in a bowl. Stir with a fork or pastry blender until the mixture is crumbly. (Make sure the streusel is in pea-sized crumbs)
* Sprinkle the streusel on top of raspberries.
* Bake for 60 minutes until a cake tester inserted in the center comes out clean. Cool and enjoy.
